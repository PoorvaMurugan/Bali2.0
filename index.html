About the Project

Vantage View

Set amidst the green expanse of Kembarampalayam, Vantage View is thoughtfully envisioned to offer a life where nature and comfort coexist. With coconut-lined avenues, landscaped walkways and open spaces that breathe, every detail is inspired by the energy of the tropics. From the pavilion-style clubhouse and resort-style pool to sustainable coconut plantations, Vantage View creates a natural retreat that feels like a permanent vacation. Whether you’re investing in a weekend getaway or building a dream farmhouse, this is where life slows down — beautifully.
